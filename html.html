The seventh Gym Leader Galarian Trainers face in their Gym Challenge. Well known for writing songs and singing as I regularly does so in Spikemuth. More often I tend to reveal his strategy to my opponents, yet i have the strength and skills to win regardless. Spikemuth is the only Gym in Galar not built over a Power Spot; previously, Rose had asked me to relocate my Gym to a faraway Power Spot, but I refused and opted to be the only Gym Leader not to use Dynamax. As a result, the town is rarely visited by fans, so I blame myself for letting my home fall into obscurity and dilapidation. I have been desperately trying to support the town with my Dark-type Pokémon but often gets frustrated with the limits of my strength. Oh well.
